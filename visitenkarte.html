Our Mission

Smirror GmbH is a young tech start-up founded in Frankfurt by four students in 2024. Our goal is to enhance smart home living with useful and simple technology. We believe that mirrors can be more than just reflections. With smart technology and voice control, we aim to help people save time and stay organized every day.
